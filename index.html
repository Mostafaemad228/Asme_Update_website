About ASME Global

ASME is a not-for-profit membership organization that enables collaboration, knowledge sharing, career enrichment, and skills development across all engineering disciplines, toward a goal of helping the global engineering community develop solutions to benefit lives and livelihoods. Founded in 1880 by a small group of leading industrialists, ASME has grown through the decades to include more than 100,000 members in 140+ countries!

About ASME HTI

ASME HTI-student section was established in 2011. ASME HTI is working in improving the student’s academic and soft skills, as well as developing their technical knowledge. We seek generating engineering graduates to keep up with the latest updates of technology in the world to help us achieve our society goals. We provide courses and competitions, as we organize national and international events and competitions.

our vision

Shaping a better future for our community by helping students conquer their way to success in their chosen occupational areas.

our mission

Foster personal, professional and academic development of students; by immersing students in excitings and challenging experiences that allow them to achieve their fullest potential.
